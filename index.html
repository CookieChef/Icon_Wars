Welcome to Icon Wars

It's you VS. the Monsters!

Rules:


Press "play" to start the game or "quit" if you'd rather leave or resart the game.

Select your source of power to defeat the monster.

You can chose:

Magic, Sword or Bow

Once you select your weapon of choice you now select your opponent

Click on the "attack" button to deal damage, but you will also take damage!

You can only attack one monster at a time.

You win if you defeat the monster before he defeats you by droping your health to 0.
